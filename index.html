MUN
Leadership is a behaviour. Not a position.

Model United Nations (MUN) is where students role play as delegates to the UN, learning about diplomacy in international relations and gaining a deeper understanding of current world issues. They develop critical thinking and ethical reasoning skills to negotiate and resolve global issues.
